**Title**: $10B+ Annual Losses, No Village-Level Forecasts

**Content**:
- Smallholder farmers lose $10B+ annually due to unpredictable weather
- Current forecasts only cover regional areas (50km+ resolution)
- No hyper-local data for village-level decisions
- Farmers rely on traditional knowledge + unreliable regional data
- Climate change making weather more extreme
